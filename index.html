ABOUT

RebadgeOnboard System Brief

Built for SuppOrg Pvt. Ltd. – a secure, role-based onboarding website for HR Transfer & Rebadging of customer resources after a new business acquisition.

Professional UI Role-Based Real-Time Tracking
USE CASE

“RebadgeOnboard” – a supplier onboarding website required for HR Transfer & Rebadging of customer resources upon a new business acquisition.

GOAL

Build a full lifecycle onboarding website for SuppOrg that manages employee transfer (rebadging), communication, offer management, and onboarding readiness in one system.

BACKGROUND (PROBLEM CONTEXT)

After a business contract is signed, SuppOrg must onboard a large number of customer employees quickly. This involves multiple steps, multiple stakeholders, and sensitive employee data.

  1. ➤ Sessions, offers, documents, onboarding
  2. ➤ HR, managers, employees (and customer HR)
  3. ➤ A centralised website to simplify employee experience
  4. ➤ Real-time connection between employee actions and HR tracking
USERS (4 ROLES)
Rebadging Employee
Checklist, document upload, clear timelines, Q&A.
HR Transition Manager
Communications, terms comparison, offers, dashboards.
Hiring/Line Manager
Manager training, assimilation and productivity support.
Customer HR / Incumbent HR
Shares HR data for due diligence and transfer prep.
USE-CASE SUMMARY
  1. 1. HR uploads employee data
  2. 2. Employees receive login access
  3. 3. Guided onboarding journey completion
  4. 4. HR monitors progress & sends reminders
  5. 5. System tracks readiness for Day 1
INTEGRATED DESIGN
12 key pages (can be reduced)
All pages support Employee + HR views where applicable.
1. Login & Authentication
2. Unified Dashboard (Role-based)
3. Employee Profile
4. Guided Onboarding Journey
5. Offer Management
6. Document Upload & Verification
7. Communication & Session
8. Task & Checklist
9. HR Monitoring Dashboard
10. Access & Tooling Guidance
11. Digital Access Request (Optional)
12. Helpdesk / Support
FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS (FR)
  • ➤ Role-Based Access Control
  • ➤ Guided Onboarding Workflow
  • ➤ Document & Offer Management
  • ➤ HR Dashboard & Progress Tracking
  • HR can track completion rates, identify delays or missing steps, and view onboarding readiness.
NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS (NFR)
  • ➤ Privacy by Design
  • ➤ Secure Authentication
  • ➤ Basic Security & Audit
EXAMPLE SCENARIO

HR uploads 150 employees / employees complete onboarding steps / 120 complete / 30 pending due to missing documents / HR identifies delays and sends reminders / dashboard shows overall readiness.
